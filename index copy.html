Love Story

1

💼 A Simple Beginning

In January 2025, Rajath, a 28-year-old graphic designer, met Srimithrra, a copywriter known for her intelligence and reserved nature, during a company branding project. Their interactions in the meeting room were limited to work matters, maintaining professionalism and proper etiquette. Unbeknownst to Rajath, Srimithrra had actually seen his design work at a national competition two years ago and admired his talent. However, true to her principles, Srimithrra maintained distance and focused only on the task, avoiding unnecessary interactions.

2

💞 Seeds of Love Through Trials

Entering February 2025, their project faced a crisis: the client rejected the initial concept and threatened to cancel the contract. Under pressure, Srimithrra came up with a creative solution that saved the project, impressing Rajath with her calmness and intelligence. Secretly, Rajath began to notice Srimithrra more deeply, especially through her social media posts filled with faith-based values and patience. Feeling there was a shared vision, Rajath performed istikharah and decided to begin taaruf. He contacted a trusted ustaz as an intermediary. A surprise emerged when Srimithrra confessed that she had also long been observing Rajath but had restrained herself until there was clear intention, an acknowledgment that warmed Rajath's heart.

3

💍 Steps Towards Allah's Blessing

The taaruf process proceeded with sincerity, with a chaperone ensuring every step followed shariah. Rajath and Srimithrra were open about their dreams of building a family blessed by Allah. However, a test came: Srimithrra's family had doubts due to Rajath's modest economic background. This is where the plot twist unfolded—Rajath presented a plan for an art-based social enterprise that he had secretly developed, showing his vision to benefit the ummah. This presentation melted Srimithrra's family's hearts. In March 2025, after istikharah and receiving family blessings, Rajath proposed to Srimithrra in a simple yet meaningful ceremony. With sincere intentions, they stepped towards marriage, confident that Allah had beautifully woven their story.
